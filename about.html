⚙️ How CarTrace Pro Works

1

Register Your Vehicle

Add your vehicle details to our secure platform. Include make, model, year, license plate, and optional VIN for enhanced protection and free Carfax Car Care integration.

2

Choose Your Protection Plan

Select from Basic ($200 reward), Gold ($300), Platinum ($500), or Diamond ($750) plans. Higher tiers offer increased community rewards and priority support.

3

Instant Community Protection

Your vehicle is immediately protected by our network of 47,892+ active members. Real-time monitoring begins instantly across our entire community.

4

Report If Stolen

If theft occurs, activate our emergency protocol with one tap. Instant push notifications alert our entire community network with your vehicle details.

Community Recovery

Members who spot your vehicle report its location. Authorities are notified, and the finder receives the reward upon successful recovery.
