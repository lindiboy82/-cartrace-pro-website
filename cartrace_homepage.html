Built from Personal Experience

👤
Lindi
Founder & CEO

Ten years ago, my car was stolen and police couldn't help me find it. Worse yet, it was used in an armed robbery. I had to find my own vehicle by driving around the city myself.

"I realized that if I could find my car, imagine what an entire community could accomplish working together."

That experience inspired me to spend the next decade creating CarTrace Pro – a community-powered vehicle recovery network that helps people find their stolen cars by crowdsourcing the search to thousands of community members who earn rewards for helping.

Today, we've achieved a 96.1% recovery rate with most vehicles found within hours, and we've paid over $1.2M in community rewards. At CarTrace Pro, you never search alone.
