How CarTrace Pro Works

Simple, community-driven vehicle recovery that benefits everyone

🚨

1. Report Instantly

Hit the emergency button and describe your stolen vehicle. Takes less than 60 seconds. Alert goes out to 47,000+ community members immediately.

👀

2. Community Watches

Thousands of eyes across Florida (expanding nationwide) watch for your vehicle while going about their day. They check license plates, make, and model.

📍

3. Location Reported

When someone spots your vehicle, they report the location with photo evidence. You get notified instantly and can alert police with exact location.

💰

4. Reward & Recovery

Once your vehicle is recovered, you pay the reward ($200-$750 based on your plan) to the helper. Everyone wins - you get your car back, they get rewarded.
